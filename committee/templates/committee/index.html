Role descriptions

  • President: Makes sure everyone knows what needs to be done, arranges committee meetings, introduces speakers
  • Vice-president: Fills in when the president is unavailable, in general helping other committee members.
  • External Secretary: Invites and coordinates with speakers, arranges speaking events
  • Internal Secretary: Manages internal society affairs, e.g. managing the membership lists
  • Treasurer: Manages the society bank account, manages reimbursements
  • Publicity Officer: Sends emails, puts up posters, manages the Facebook Page
  • Events Managers: Organises all social events, usually 2 people are in this role so they can share the workload
  • Corporate Officer: Manages relationships with corporate sponsors and sponsor events.
  • Webmaster: Keeps the website updated and maintained
  • General members: Helps out with events
