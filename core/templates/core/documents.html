College Societies

The following societies were once recognized by the the Archimedenas, but no longer exist:

New Pythagoreans

This was the society for Christ’s College, Downing College, Emmanuel College, Girton College, Gonville and Caius College, Homerton College, Hughes Hall, Jesus College, Sidney Sussex College, Trinity Hall and Anglia Polytechnic University (formerly Cambridge College of Arts and Technology). No electronic copy of its constitution is available.

Quintics (Quintic Society)

This was the society for Corpus Christi College, Darwin College, Newnham College, Pembroke College, Peterhouse, Queens’ College, St. Catharine’s College, Selwyn College and Wolfson College.

  • Rules of 1925 as amended up to 1987: text or PDF

Tensors (Tensors Mathematical Society)

This was the society for Churchill College, Clare College, Clare Hall, Fitzwilliam College, King’s College, Lucy Cavendish College, Magdalene College, New Hall, Robinson College and St. Edmund’s College.

  • Constitution (undated)
